Here's my interactive story!



I'm so proud of this project. I spent so many hours figuring out tiny details. Working in Photoshop, most of my images were extremely pixelated as a result of the small dimensions, but the quality is much better from afar in the completed project. Around 90% of my work time was in Photoshop. Creating the image maps and coding the new website came the most easily to me in this project. However, I really enjoyed the challenge of creating the images from scratch to form a cohesive story. I usually avoid focusing on the more artistic side of projects, but I took the opportunity to try and outdo myself with this assignment. I used quite a few images from online, but I also created elements of pages entirely on my own (I'm most proud of the hallway when walking towards the lasers, the bank in the title screen, and the security guards). I tried my best to minimize my use of outside images and create as much as I could from scratch, but I found that many elements of my story needed extra help to be communicated properly to players.
