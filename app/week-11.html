Week 11

Fast Food Survey Results

This is the infographic I came up with! I had to do a lot of improvising this week because I was unable to go to the campus and the iPad version of Illustrator was missing some key features for this assignment, including the graph feature. I ended up multiplying each value from the survey with a constant to create the size of each rectangle, so both graphs are proportional and consistent with each other. I changed the color of the rectangles to gradients that matched each restaurant's color scheme, and added extra rankings on the side. They're not too scientific in the information they're conveying -- I chose the "most popular restaurants" by using the obvious top three choices in the first question, then breaking the tie between second and third place by evaluating the votes on the second question. "Worst for your wallet" was even less scientific. I ended up looking for restaurants that had higher numbers in the first question and lower numbers in the second question. Specifically, five Burger King customers took the survey, but not one chose it as the best value. Those were the factors I took in with the rankings. I also created the gold, silver, bronze, and participant medals, and I'm really happy with how they came out. Overall, both my survey questions and the layout of the infographic are pretty basic, but I tried to add some flare in the details of the design as well as some individuality in how the information itself is presented.
