Here's the first concrete poem I made! I wanted the street light to show its illumination at full opacity, and to put an effect on the text to show that it's being affected by the light. I didn't have a specific goal with how it would turn out so I tried different designs until I settled on this one. I really like the contrast between the white and black text. The text splicing was difficult to figure out and I couldn't change the font on either of my pieces but I think the message is clear. I wanted the art to match how dramatic the poem was, and to capture some symbolism in that severing ties with someone can make you remember that there's a whole world beyond what you can currently see.
