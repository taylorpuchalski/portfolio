    • Weighing In


    Weighing In is a personal blog I created with the goal of strenghtening my informal writing skills. It is a platform for the critical examination of film and television, driven by my desire to enhance my analytical abilities and gain a comprehensive understanding of the fundamentals of the entertainment industry. My goal is to offer insightful, well-reasoned reviews and analyses. I aim to encourage discussion about the state of film and television and how its impact is reflected in current events. Through Weighing In, I seek to share my passion and drive conversations about art that makes an impact on both the big and small screens. This platform serves as a testament to my commitment to lifelong learning and my dedication to the captivating world of film and television.
