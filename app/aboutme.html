    • Resume/About Me

    In my first semester of college, I rejected the idea of pursuing a career in entertainment. Although it seemed like the best fit for me and my passion for bringing people together through art, it seemed impossible to thrive in such a competitive industry. Eventually I decided that I wouldn't know if success in entertainment was possible unless I gave it a fair shot. I ended up transferring schools and began pursuing a media studies degree. Ever since, I've been ensuring I take every opportunity to create meaningful projects. With my art, I intend to start conversations and connect people together through our shared values and experiences. I value the connections I've made through my shared interests more than anything and, as a result, I aim to promote those connections in others. I'm motivated by the idea of contributing to projects that captivate and encourage discussion. My first internship with BronxNet Community Television allowed me to learn the basics of video production and creating for TV. Through my second position, an internship with the nonprofit JCHHOMES, I was able to work directly with social media, focusing on creating short-form video content for promotional posts. In my third internship, marketing at Adam Leitman Bailey, P.C., I've utilized my short-form content creation skills in a corporate setting.
