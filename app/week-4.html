Collage Two

Here's the second collage I made! The theme of this one was a look back on the highlights of the last year or so. Pictures in this collage include me and my boyfriend, a significant gift box (which is actually green in the original image), the envelope from when I sent mail from the first time, and a shot from a Megan The Stallion show I attended some months ago. I used the "subtract" blend mode for the gift box and "luminosity" on the envelope. The background images in both collages were taken in the same place, about five minutes apart. I didn't have a particular goal with blending, I just tried out different ones and chose what I thought looked cool.
